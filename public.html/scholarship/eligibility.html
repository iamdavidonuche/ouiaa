OUIAA SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAMME

To ensure that the scholarship reaches students who are both in need and capable of making the most of the opportunity, applicants must meet the following criteria:

Eligibility Requirements

General Eligibility Criteria

  • Be currently enrolled as a full-time undergraduate student at Oduduwa University.
  • Must be in a 200-level or higher level at the time of application. Students in 100-level are not eligible.
  • Have a minimum CGPA of 3.5 on a 5.0 scale from the most recent academic session.
  • Be able to provide a clear and detailed combined financial and personal impact statement, outlining why the scholarship is needed and how it will benefit their academic journey.
